⛏️ Blocky World

A tiny creative-mode sandbox

How to play

  • ⬆ walks forward, ⤒ jumps (right next to it).
  • Drag the screen to look around and turn (or A/D).
  • Pick an item from the hotbar — it goes in your hand.
  • Point the + at a tree or 🍎 apple and tap to grab it — even with a block in your hand.
  • Want to build? Tap the ground, or press Place to build anywhere (even onto a tree).
  • Empty hand or pickaxe? Tap the ground (or Mine) to dig.
  • Punch trees for wood. Craft sticks, a table & a pickaxe.
  • Stone & ores need a pickaxe. Animals can't be hurt 🐾.
  • Eat 🍎 apples and 🍉 watermelons so you don't starve!
